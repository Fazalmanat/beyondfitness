Leg Workout

Start your leg training with these beginner-friendly exercises.

A beginner leg workout focuses on building foundational strength and muscle endurance in the lower body. It typically includes exercises such as leg presses, lying hamstring curls, standing stiff leg deadlifts, and both standing and seated calf raises. This routine targets the major muscle groups in the legs—quadriceps, hamstrings, and calves—making it ideal for newcomers to strength training. Emphasizing proper form and controlled movements, this workout helps develop a solid base for more advanced leg training in the future.

Leg Workout Breakdown

  • Leg Press
    • Sets: 3
    • Reps: 10-12
  • Lying Hamstring Curls
    • Sets: 3
    • Reps: 10-12
  • Standing Stiff Leg Deadlifts
    • Sets: 3
    • Reps: 10-12
  • Standing Calf Raise
    • Sets: 3
    • Reps: 12-15
  • Seated Calf Raise
    • Sets: 3
    • Reps: 12-15

Additional Information

This comprehensive program effectively targets the quads, hamstrings, and calves, making it ideal for beginners.

Rest: 60-90 seconds between sets.
