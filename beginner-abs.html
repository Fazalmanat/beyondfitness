Start Strengthening Your Core

Beginner-friendly exercises for your abs.

A beginner abs workout is designed to strengthen the core using fundamental exercises. It includes movements like jack knives, crunches, scissors kicks, heel touches, and planks, targeting various abdominal muscles. The routine emphasizes proper form and controlled movements, making it suitable for newcomers. With sets and reps included, this workout helps build a solid foundation for future core training, improving overall stability and strength. This structured approach encourages consistency and gradual progression in fitness levels.

Beginner Abs Workout

  • Jack Knives
    • Sets: 3
    • Reps: 10-12
  • Abs Crunches
    • Sets: 3
    • Reps: 12-15
  • Scissors Kicks
    • Sets: 3
    • Duration: 15-20 seconds
  • Heel Touches
    • Sets: 3
    • Reps: 12-15 (per side)
  • Low Plank
    • Sets: 3
    • Duration: 20-30 seconds
  • Side Tucks
    • Sets: 3
    • Reps: 10-12 (per side)
  • Bicycle Kicks
    • Sets: 3
    • Reps: 12-15 (per side)
  • Side Plank
    • Sets: 3
    • Duration: 15-20 seconds (per side)

Additional Information

This beginner abs workout effectively targets the entire core, building foundational strength and endurance.

Rest: 60-90 seconds between sets.

Tip: Focus on proper form and engage your core throughout each exercise.
