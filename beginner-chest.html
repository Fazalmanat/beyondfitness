Get Started with Simple Chest Workouts

Build chest strength with these beginner-friendly exercises.

This video is a beginner-friendly chest workout guide that explains exercises to build chest strength and muscle definition. It features various exercises tailored for those new to working out, demonstrating proper form and technique.
The video provides step-by-step instructions in Hindi, covering essential chest exercises, including variations of push-ups and dumbbell exercises that target the upper, middle, and lower chest muscles. It also includes tips on repetitions, rest times, and tips to avoid common mistakes for effective results.

Chest Workout Breakdown

  • Push-ups
    • Sets: 3
    • Reps: 12-15
  • Incline Push-ups
    • Sets: 3
    • Reps: 12-15
  • Dumbbell Bench Press
    • Sets: 3
    • Reps: 10-12
  • Incline Dumbbell Press
    • Sets: 3
    • Reps: 10-12
  • Dumbbell Flys
    • Sets: 3
    • Reps: 12
  • Dips
    • Sets: 3
    • Reps: To failure

Additional Information

Each exercise targets different parts of the chest, helping beginners develop chest strength and form.

Rest: 60-90 seconds between sets.
