Ultimate Core Challenge

Advanced ab exercises to maximize your strength.

An expert abs workout targets advanced core strength and endurance with high-intensity movements. It includes scissors, pendulums, leg holds, crunches, hip raises, and challenging variations like leg raise negatives, full plank knees, plank side knees, and plank jumps. Bicycle exercises and finishers add extra core engagement. This workout focuses on the upper, lower, and oblique muscles, building a strong, defined core through diverse exercises that require stability, balance, and control. Ideal for experienced athletes, it maximizes core strength and muscle endurance.

Additional Information

This expert abs workout targets the upper, lower, and oblique muscles of the core, enhancing overall strength, endurance, and definition.

Rest: 60-90 seconds between sets.

Tip: Focus on proper form, engage your core, and adjust reps/duration according to fitness level.
